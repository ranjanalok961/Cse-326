Motivational thought for students

“The beautiful thing
about learning is that
no one can take it
away from you.”
 “A person
who never made a mistake
never tried anything new." 		“Education
is the most powerful
weapon you can use
to change the world.”
“You are braver
than you believe,
stronger than you seem
and
smarter than you think.” 		“Never let
the fear of striking
out stop you
from playing the game.”
“The future belongs to those
who believe
in the beauty of their dreams.” 		“Learn from yesterday.
Live for today.
Hope for tomorrow.” 		“He who asks a question
is a fool for five minutes;
he who does not ask a question
remains a fool forever.”
