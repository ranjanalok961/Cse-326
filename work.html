Motivational thought for work

“You’ve got to get up
every morning with determination
if you’re going to go
to bed with satisfaction.” 		“Go as far
as you can see;
when you get there,
you’ll be able to see further.” 		“Your talent determines
what you can do.
Your motivation determines
how much you’re willing to do.
Your attitude determines
how well you do it.”
“The individual
who says it is not possible
should move out of
the way of those doing it.” 		“When someone tells me ‘no,’
it doesn’t mean
I can’t do it,
it simply means
I can’t do it with them.”
“Build your own dreams
or
someone else will hire you
to build theirs.” 		“Never give up on a dream
just because of the time
it will take to accomplish it.
The time will pass anyway.” 		“The only way
to do great work is
to love what you do.
If you haven’t found it yet,
keep looking.
Don’t settle.”
