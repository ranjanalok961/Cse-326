Motivational thought for employee

Whether you think you can,
or you think you can’t
– you’re right. 		I know not all that may be coming,
but be it what it will,
I’ll go to it laughing. 		The sun himself is weak
when he first rises,
and gathers strength
and
courage as the day gets on.
How wonderful it is
that nobody need wait
a single moment before
starting to improve the world. 		If I had nine hours
to chop down a tree,
I’d spend the first six
sharpening my axe.
Talent wins games,
but teamwork and intelligence
wins championships. 		“Always be a first-rate
version of yourself,
instead of a second-rate
version of somebody else. ” 		The only way
to achieve the impossible
is to believe
it is possible.
