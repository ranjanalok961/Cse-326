Motivational thought for study

“Success is no accident.
It is hard work,
perseverance,
learning,
studying, sacrifice and most of all,
love of what you are doing
or
learning to do.” 		“There are no secrets
to success.
It is the result of preparation,
hard work,
and
learning from failure.” 		“However difficult life may seem,
there is always something
you can do and succeed at.”
“Striving for success
without hard work is
like trying to harvest
where you haven’t planted.” 		“Success
is the sum of small efforts,
repeated day in and day out.”
“Success isn’t overnight.
It’s when every day
you get a little better
than the day before.
It all adds up.” 		“The secret of success
is to do
the common things uncommonly well.” 		“Failure
is the opportunity
to begin
again more intelligently.”
